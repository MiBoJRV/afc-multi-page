What’s the Anti-Fraud Centre?

Financial fraud has become an unfortunate reality for many people. We started the Anti-Fraud Centre 5 years ago, as we witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of these deceptive practices.

Our Mission

Every day, we receive numerous reports detailing the toll of online financial fraud. Whether it's through sophisticated scams or deceitful tactics, individuals find themselves ensnared in deception. That is why we ran a questionnaire to study the frequency and nature of financial fraud instances.
